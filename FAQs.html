FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


General Info:

Welcome to the General Information section of our FAQs! Here, we've compiled answers to some of the most commonly asked questions about our company, services, and policies. If you have a question that isn't answered here, please don't hesitate to contact us directly, and our team will be happy to assist you.

Trail Info:

Q- What type of trails do we offer?

A- We offer 4 main types of trails. Blue (moderate), Red (Difficult), Black (Severe) and Double-black (extreme).

Q- Are the trails suitable for children?

A- Yes the Blue (moderate) trails is suitable for childrem. We also provide a Intermidiate skills area.

Q- What do the RED and AMBER weather warnings mean?

A- Amber: Mountain Wheel Adventures intends to open but there is a high likelihood of bad weather which could potentially disrupt your visit.
Red: Extreme weather is expected and the trail network and uplift will be closed. If the traffic light system shows Red for the current / following day, all customers who have pre-booked can choose to either receive a refund or to move their booking to another available date via the reception team.

Q- Do we provide Trail Maps?

A- Yes, we provide free detailed maps for everyone before accesing the trails.

Bike Rental Info:

Q- What type of bikes do we offer?

A- We offer 3 types of bike for rental. Two versatile mountain bikes and one hardtail mountain bike.

Q- Can i reserve bikes in advance?

Yes. You can easily reserve your bike online or by contacting our team directly. Walk-in rentals are also available, subject to availability.

Q- Do you provide helmets and other safety gear?

Yes. We proivide helmets, gloves, and knee pads for free.

Booking and Payments Info:

Q- How do i book a bike or Ticket Pass?

You can easily reserve your bike or ticket pass online or by contacting our team directly by visiting the Contact us page.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We strive to make your experience as convenient as possible. We accept the following forms of payment: Credit Cards, Debit Cards, Cash and Contactless Payment.
