Bikes

Our selection of high-quality mountain bike rentals caters to riders of all ages and abilities, offering a wide range of bikes from lightweight hardtails to full-suspension rigs. Our fleet is designed to handle various terrains and riding styles, making it an ideal way to experience mountain biking without owning your own bike. So grab your helmet, pack snacks, and hit the trails on one of our top-of-the-line mountain bikes.

Under 18's Info

Mountain Wheel Adventures prioritizes the safety and well-being of its customers, particularly minors. They require all individuals under 18 to be accompanied and supervised by a parent or legal guardian at all times while participating in any activities or services provided by the company. This ensures proper supervision and guidance, minimizes accident risks, and adheres to safety guidelines. By participating in activities such as bike rentals, guided tours, or trail access, individuals under 18 agree to be accompanied and supervised by a parent or guardian. Mountain Wheel Adventures appreciates cooperation in ensuring the safety of all participants and looks forward to providing a safe and enjoyable experience.
