About Us

Mountain Wheel Adventures is a passionate mountain biking and outdoor adventure company founded to provide unforgettable experiences for riders of all ages and skill levels. With a dedicated team of professionals, they create and maintain top-notch trails that offer excitement, challenge, and scenic beauty. They are more than just a trail company; they are a community of outdoor enthusiasts who share a common love for exploration and adventure. Join them as they pedal through forests, conquer challenging terrain, and soak in nature, creating unforgettable memories.

Why choose us

Mountain Wheel Adventures offers a unique mountain biking experience with expertly designed trails, safety-first practices, and exceptional service. We provide well-marked trails, safety gear rentals, and experienced staff to ensure a safe and enjoyable ride. The community atmosphere is vibrant, with like-minded riders connecting and making lasting memories. The trails are conveniently located, surrounded by stunning natural beauty, offering a convenient escape from city life. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced rider, Mountain Wheel Adventures is dedicated to providing unforgettable experiences, leaving you wanting more. Choose Mountain Wheel Adventures for an unparalleled mountain biking experience.

Company Values

Mountain Wheel Adventures is a company that values integrity, professionalism, and environmental stewardship. Our core values include a passion for adventure, safety, inclusivity, community engagement, continuous improvement, environmental sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We prioritize the safety of riders, staff, and the environment, and strive to minimize their impact. We welcome riders of all backgrounds and skill levels, and actively engage with the local community. We also prioritize sustainable trail design and conservation efforts. Our commitment to customer satisfaction ensures an enjoyable and memorable experience for all riders.
